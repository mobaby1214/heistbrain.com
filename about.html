Built by creators who got tired of the grind.

Heist was born out of frustration. We were doing content creation ourselves — stitching together four different tools, re-explaining our brand to AI every session, losing hours to the formatting-scheduling-posting cycle. So we built the tool we wished existed. One Brain that remembers everything. One place for everything else.

Why Heist Exists

Content creation shouldn't feel like a second job. But for most creators and business owners, it does. You spend your mornings writing, your afternoons reformatting, and your evenings wondering if any of it actually worked. Then you do it all again next week.

Every AI tool on the market forgets you the moment you close the tab. You explain your voice, your audience, your industry — and then you explain it again tomorrow. That's not a tool working for you. That's you working for the tool.

Business owners shouldn't need to become prompt engineers to sound like themselves online. That's why we built the Brain — a 10-layer memory system that learns who you are, what works, and what your brand actually sounds like. Not a template. Not a one-size-fits-all voice selector. A system that gets smarter every time you use it.

Heist exists to give you back the hours you're losing — with an AI that actually remembers who you are.
