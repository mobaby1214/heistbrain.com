Frequently asked questions

How many hashtags should I use per post?

Instagram: 3-5 niche hashtags beat 30 generic ones in most 2026 tests. TikTok: 3-4, with at least one trending. LinkedIn: 3 max. X: 1-2. The days of hashtag stuffing are over.

Should I use mega or niche hashtags?

A mix. Mega hashtags get you visibility but low conversion. Niche hashtags get you engaged viewers. Use 1-2 mega, 1-2 medium, 1-2 niche — not all 30.

Do hashtags still work in 2026?

On Instagram and TikTok, yes — they're how discovery works. On LinkedIn and Facebook, they help slightly. On X, they barely matter anymore. Prioritize accordingly.
