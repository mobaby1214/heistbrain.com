Frequently asked questions

How does the brand voice analyzer work?

It runs entirely in your browser. The tool counts words and sentences, calculates the Flesch Reading Ease score, estimates formality based on word choice patterns, and extracts your most-used meaningful words. No data leaves your device.

How much text should I paste?

3-5 paragraphs (300-800 words) gives the most reliable profile. Less than 150 words will be noisy; more than 2000 is fine but overkill.

What's the best way to use my voice profile?

Copy the summary and tags into a brand voice document. Paste that into any AI tool at the start of a prompt, or — if you're using Heist — save it to your Brain so every generation uses it automatically.
