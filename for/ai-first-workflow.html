It's not magic — it's MCP

MCP stands for Model Context Protocol. It's the standard that lets AI assistants connect to outside tools safely — with permissions you control and an audit log of everything the AI does. Heist is one of the most complete MCP integrations in content marketing: 56+ tools your AI can call, covering everything from drafting a single post to auditing your entire brand brain. Every action requires a reason. Every write creates a log entry. You're in control — your AI just types faster than you do.
