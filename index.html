FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

How is Heist different from ChatGPT? +
ChatGPT generates text. Heist generates a system — with brand memory that persists, platform-specific previews, campaign organization, and scheduling. It's the difference between a tool and a workflow.
How does the Brand Brain work? +
You define your voice, audience, and offer during onboarding. The Brain stores this permanently and applies it to every piece of content. The longer you use Heist, the more it sounds like you.
Does AI content sound robotic? +
Not when it has a Brain. Most AI tools are stateless — they forget you after every session. Your Brain learns from you and applies your voice, tone, and style to every single post.
What platforms does Heist support? +
LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube — six platforms from one dashboard. TikTok and YouTube support native video uploads. The Founder and Agency plans also include blog content generation.
Can I try it before I pay? +
Yes. Every plan includes a 7-day free trial with full Pro features. No credit card required. Set up your Brain and see for yourself.
What if I need more posts? +
You can purchase generation credit packs starting at $9 for 50 extra posts, or upgrade your plan at any time. No long-term commitments.
What happens after my trial ends? +
You'll be moved to the Starter plan. No surprise charges.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, cancel from Settings. No fees, no questions asked.
Do I own the content Heist creates? +
Absolutely. Everything generated is 100% yours.
Is there an API? +
Not yet, but it's on our roadmap. Let us know at support@heistbrain.com.
Can I manage multiple brands or clients? +
Yes. The Agency plan ($199/mo) supports up to 25 Brand Brains, each with its own voice, memory, and analytics — completely isolated. You also get 10 team seats with role-based access (Owner, Admin, Editor, Viewer) and content approval workflows.
Does Heist support video content? +
Yes. Upload videos directly to TikTok and YouTube from Heist. The Brain generates optimized captions, descriptions, tags, and hashtags for each platform. Chunked uploads with pause and resume — even large files.
How does the auto-learning work? +
Every 6 hours, Heist pulls engagement data from your connected platforms (Meta, X, LinkedIn). Posts that outperform your average by 2x are automatically identified, and the patterns that made them work are added to your Brain as insight facts. Your next generation is informed by what actually performed — not guesswork.
Is my content safe? Can I undo mistakes? +
Yes on both. Deleted content goes to Trash and is recoverable for 30 days. Every edit saves the previous version — you can revert to any point in history. Drafts autosave every 30 seconds. Your database is backed up every 6 hours with integrity verification. And all data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM.
